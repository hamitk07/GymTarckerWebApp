Personal Plan

Designed for personal usage

The Personal Plan is designed for users who want to train individually. With this plan, you can access customized workout and nutrition programs, log your daily activities, and track your progress step by step. It’s the perfect choice for anyone who prefers to move at their own pace and enjoy a fully independent fitness experience.

Coach Plan

Designed for trainers or team coaches

The Coach Plan is built for personal trainers and team coaches. With this plan, coaches can easily monitor their athletes’ workouts, performance data, and progress from a single dashboard. They can create and share tailored programs for individuals or teams, and provide instant feedback to boost motivation and performance. It’s a powerful management and tracking tool for every coach who wants to train and guide athletes more effectively.
